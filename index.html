About Tech Day 2025

A day to celebrate innovation, learning, and the incredible talent at our India site.

Tech Day 2025 is our flagship annual technology showcase, bringing together the brightest minds across engineering, product, design, and data at our India site. This year's event features live presentations, interactive poster sessions, lightning talks, panel discussions, and recognition of outstanding technical contributions.

Whether you're presenting groundbreaking research, showcasing a side project, or simply here to learn — Tech Day is your platform. We encourage every team member to participate, ask questions, and be inspired by the innovation happening right here.

Join us for a day of exploration, collaboration, and celebration of technology that matters.
